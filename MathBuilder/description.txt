Math Builder - Visual Math Game for Young Children

A colorful, interactive math app designed for 5-year-olds to learn basic addition and subtraction through visual blocks on the screen.

Features:
- Large, easy-to-tap buttons perfect for small hands
- Visual blocks or circles that appear and disappear
- Clear number display at the top of the screen
- Slow, smooth animations for easy understanding
- Bright, cheerful colors to keep children engaged
- Perfect for learning on a TV or large screen
- Practice counting and basic math concepts

How to use:
1. Press the green Add button to make blocks appear (+1)
2. Press the red Subtract button to make blocks disappear (-1)
3. Watch the number change at the top
4. Reset to start over

Maximum: 20 blocks to keep it manageable for young learners
